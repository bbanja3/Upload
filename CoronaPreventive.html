Protect yourself and others from the spread of COVID-19

You could spread COVID-19 to others even if you do not feel sick. You can take some simple precautions to reduce the chances of being infected or spread COVID-19:

  • Wash hands often
  • Avoid going to crowed places
  • Keep 6 feet distance between eachother
  • Stay home and self-isolate even if you show minor symptoms
  • Wear mask and gloves everytime you leave your home


Clean and disinfect

Clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces daily This includes phones, doorknobs, car doors,tables, handles, toilets, sink, etc. Use detergent or soap and water prior to disinfection.
