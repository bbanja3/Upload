(Contact your healthcare provider immediately if you have any of these symptoms)
People with covid have has a wide range of symptoms reported- ranging from mild to severe illness. These symptoms may appear 2-14 days after exposure to the virus.

Here are the symptoms indicating to COVID-19 and when you should seek emergency medical attention:

  • Cough
  • Fever
  • Chills
  • Sore throat
  • Shortness of breath
  • Loss of taste or smell
